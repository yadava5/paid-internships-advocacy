Our Mission

Why this matters

"To advocate for fair compensation in internships, ensuring every student has equal access to career-building opportunities regardless of their economic background."

The Problem

Unpaid internships create barriers for students from low-income backgrounds, perpetuating inequality in professional opportunities.

The Solution

Through awareness, data, and advocacy, we can push for policy changes and employer accountability.

The Goal

A future where every internship provides fair compensation, creating an equitable path to career success.
